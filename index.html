SPEL

Space and Planetary Exploration Laboratory

Space and Planetary Exploration Laboratory is dedicated to research and develop instrumentation for geoscience and aerospace technologies. A place where to combine the geosciences and astronomy-instrumentation knowledge to develop novel techniques and instruments to improve quality of life and safety of the population together with contributing in the next step of the mankind: colonization of other planets.

Chile has wide experience in geosciences, being the global capital of astronomy due his excellent geographical conditions and highly competent professionals. The main goal of the laboratory is take the existing acknowledge in our competing areas and apply this solutions in mining, geology, seismic and atmospheric problems.
